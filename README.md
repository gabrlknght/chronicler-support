## ✅ Verified Buyer Support

Buyers receive a **`verified-buyer`** label on their Discussion threads, marking them for priority support.

**To get verified:**
1. Open a new Discussion in the appropriate category
2. Include your **PayPal Transaction ID** in your first post (format: `17S00000000000000`)
3. That's it — your thread will be labeled within 1–2 business days

You can find your Transaction ID in your PayPal confirmation email or under *Activity* in your PayPal account.

> 🔒 Your transaction ID is only used for verification. It is not stored anywhere or shared with third parties.

## 🐛 Bug Report Checklist

A good bug report gets fixed faster. Before submitting, please have ready:

- [ ] Your **Chronicler version** (found in *Appearance → Themes* in your WP dashboard)
- [ ] Your **WordPress version**
- [ ] Your **PHP version** (found in *Tools → Site Health*)
- [ ] A **clear description** of what you expected vs. what happened
- [ ] **Steps to reproduce** the issue
- [ ] A **screenshot or screen recording** if applicable
- [ ] Any **error messages** from the browser console or WP debug log

## 📦 Updates

Theme updates are announced in the [📢 Announcements](../../discussions/categories/announcements) category.

**To update your theme:**
1. Watch this repo (top right → *Watch → Custom → Discussions*) to get notified of new releases
2. Re-download the latest version using the link in your original purchase confirmation email
3. Install via *Appearance → Themes → Add New → Upload Theme* and confirm the overwrite

> ⚠️ Always back up your site before updating.
