# 👋 Welcome to Chronicler Support — Start Here

Welcome to the official support board for **Chronicler**, the WordPress theme for tabletop RPG campaigns.

This is the right place to report bugs, ask questions, request features, and share what you've built.

---

## 🗺️ How this board works

**Categories:**
- 🐛 **Bug Reports** — something broken? Post here with your version info
- ❓ **How Do I...** — usage and customization questions
- 💡 **Feature Requests** — ideas for future versions
- 🎲 **Show & Tell** — share your Chronicler-powered site
- 📢 **Announcements** — theme updates and release notes (posted by maintainer only)

---

## ✅ Getting your Verified Buyer label

If you purchased Chronicler, include your **PayPal Transaction ID** in your first post. I'll add the `verified-buyer` label to your thread, marking it for priority support.

Your Transaction ID is in your PayPal confirmation email — it looks like `17S00000000000000`. It's only used to confirm your purchase and never stored or shared.

---

## 📦 Staying up to date

**Watch this repo** to get notified when updates drop:
- Click **Watch** (top right of the repo page)
- Select **Custom → Discussions**
- Check **New discussion** under the Announcements category

Updates are re-downloaded using the link in your original purchase confirmation email.
